# Campus photo shot list — Seminarul Teologic Ortodox Târgoviște

A practical brief for the campus photo shoot. The goal: one strong **hero** image per place + a couple of **detail** shots, plus a **360°** capture for the future virtual tour. Deliver the files into `prototype/assets/` (or share them) and the site is ready to drop them in.

---

## General guidelines

- **Light:** exteriors look best in soft morning / late-afternoon light or under light overcast — avoid harsh midday shadows. For interiors, turn on **all** the lights, shoot toward (not into) windows, and use a **tripod** for the dim rooms.
- **Gear:** a DSLR/mirrorless or a recent phone is fine. A **tripod** for interiors and 360s. A **360° camera** (Insta360, Ricoh Theta, etc.) for the walk-through tours — even a phone 360 app works to start.
- **Framing:** shoot **landscape (3:2 / 16:9)** for web. Keep verticals straight (level the camera — buildings shouldn't lean). For each place: 1 wide *establishing* shot + 2–3 closer *detail* shots.
- **People:** a few candid shots with students/teachers (with consent) bring it to life — but also capture **clean, empty** versions of each space.
- **Consistency:** try to shoot all exteriors in similar weather/time so the set feels cohesive.
- **Files:** highest quality JPEG (or RAW), **≥ 3000 px** on the long side. Name them clearly: `capela-1.jpg`, `clase-1.jpg`, etc.
